\chapter*{Abstract}
\label{chap:abstract}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{\nameref{chap:abstract}}

TermTem is an application designed to reduce communication barriers between people with hearing disabilities and hearing individuals in Thailand. Communication difficulties often occur because many Thai people do not understand Thai Sign Language, making everyday interactions challenging for members of the deaf community. The objective of this project is to develop a system that facilitates communication through multiple interaction methods, including sign language recognition, speech recognition, and conversational AI.

The proposed system integrates several machine learning components such as sign language recognition, speech recognition, natural language understanding, and text-to-animation generation. These technologies allow the system to interpret sign language gestures from video input, convert speech into text, and generate responses through speech or sign language animations (Avatar).

TermTem aims to provide near real time communication assistance for hearing disabilities users, individuals learning Thai sign language, and general users who need to communicate with deaf individuals. By applying artificial intelligence technologies, this project seeks to improve accessibility, support communication, and enhance the quality of life for people with hearing disabilities in Thailand.
